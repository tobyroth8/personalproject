Michigan Equity Research

MERC will consist of a variety of teams each overseen by one or more members of the E-Board. The Outreach Team will be in charge of club recruiting efforts and alumni relations. The Education Team will be in charge of Pupil education. Client Teams will consist of a Senior Analyst, an Associate, and two Analysts each assigned to providing investment consulting advice to their respective client. Industry and Economic Teams will consist of a Senior Analyst, an Associate, and two Analysts each assigned to producing research reports on either the economy or equities within specific industries.

What is the structure?

All members enter the club as Pupils, where they undergo a semester of club orientation, technical training, and professional development. After passing a final examination, they graduate to become club Analysts, where they conduct research on teams to offer (1) periodic economic and equity reports or (2) investment consulting projects for clients. At any point as an analyst, a member has the opportunity to run for club leadership, with elections held in December of each year. The entire club votes on the Managing Director, Director of Internal Affairs, and Director of External Affairs and this newly-elected E-Board then assigns Associates and Senior Analysts to different project teams. These lower leadership positions are held for one semester whereas E-Board positions are held for an entire year (December to December).
